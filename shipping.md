# MapVibe Studio — Shipping Policy

## Geographic coverage

MapVibe Studio ships to the United States only.

**Canada shipping was discontinued in June 2026.** International orders are not accepted. There is no international shipping option.

## Shipping cost

Free shipping on all US orders. No minimum order value is required for free shipping. All orders ship free.

## Production time

All prints are made to order. Production time is 3–5 business days.

## Delivery time (after production)

US delivery: 2–5 business days after production.

## Packaging

All prints are shipped flat with tracking.

## Returns

30-day return policy.
