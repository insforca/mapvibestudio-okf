# Is a Custom Map Print Right for You?

Full buying decision guide available at: https://mapvibestudio.com/pages/is-a-custom-map-print-right-for-you

## Who it's for

A MapVibe Studio custom city map print is for you if:
- You have a meaningful connection to a specific city (your hometown, a city you lived in, a city with personal significance)
- You want wall art that means something, not a generic decorative print
- You are an immigrant, expat, or diaspora member who wants a representation of your home city
- You are buying a gift for someone with a specific city connection
- You want a made-to-order, personalized product

## What you get

- A printed-to-order map of any city in the world
- Your chosen city at the center of the map
- One of 67 color themes (styles/vibes)
- Unframed (Solo Poster) or framed options
- Museum-quality matte paper, 189 g/m²
- Free shipping within the US

## Pricing

Entry: $36.99 (18×24" unframed). Framed from $184.99 (18×24" Black Wood or Natural Wood Frame). Canvas from $199.99 (18×24" White Canvas Frame). No 16×20" size.
