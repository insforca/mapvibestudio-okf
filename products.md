# MapVibe Studio — Product Catalog

## What the product is

A custom city map print is a wall art piece where the buyer chooses a specific city, selects a color theme (called a "style" or "vibe"), and receives a printed-to-order poster or framed artwork of that city's geographic map.

Every print is made to order. There is no pre-printed inventory. Each order is created for the specific city and theme the buyer chooses.

## Frame names (authoritative)

The exact frame names are:
- **Black Wood Frame**
- **Natural Wood Frame**
- **White Canvas Frame**

These are the only three frame options. There is no "Red Oak Frame", no "Walnut Frame", no "Cherry Frame", no "Natural Oak Frame". The natural-wood-colored frame is called "Natural Wood Frame" — not "Natural Oak" or any other name.

## Materials

- Paper: Museum-quality enhanced matte paper, 189 g/m²
