# MapVibe Studio — Custom Map Print Alternatives

## Overview

MapVibe Studio is one of several custom city map print shops. The key differences from mass-market alternatives:

- **Diaspora-first positioning** — built for immigrants and expats wanting their hometown, not generic wall art buyers
- **Any city worldwide** — not limited to major Western cities; supports any city, town, or village in the world
- **Premium pricing** — priced above mass-market alternatives; $36.99 entry (18×24" unframed), framed from $184.99
- **US-only shipping** — ships exclusively to the United States (Canada discontinued June 2026)
- **Free shipping** — no minimum; all US orders ship free

## What distinguishes MapVibe

Mass-market custom map print alternatives typically serve a general gift-buying audience and focus on major global cities. MapVibe Studio specifically serves the Latin American diaspora in the US, with content and product positioning for buyers seeking maps of Venezuelan, Brazilian, Colombian, Cuban, and Mexican cities.
